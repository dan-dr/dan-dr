# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Builders, founders, open-source maintainers, collaborators, and hiring or funding peers evaluating Dan's work and interests from his GitHub profile.

## Product Purpose

Give visitors a fast, credible picture of what Dan builds, what he is actively working on, where he contributes, and how to reach him.

## Brand Personality

Builder-first, restless, and direct. The profile should feel active and technically credible without becoming a badge collection or résumé wall.

## Anti-references

Do not clone another GitHub profile, default to the common blue developer-profile template, or bury the useful work beneath decorative widgets and generic claims.

## Design Principles

- Lead with a clear identity and a memorable point of view.
- Show active work and shipped contributions instead of making abstract claims.
- Keep every section scannable in GitHub's constrained README renderer.
- Use personality in the framing; use precision in the project descriptions.
- Make contact paths immediate and redundant only where useful.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Maintain readable contrast in light and dark GitHub themes, descriptive alternative text, semantic headings, and a usable experience when animation is reduced or unavailable.
